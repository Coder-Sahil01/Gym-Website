Fitness Styles

Strength Training

Build muscle and increase your strength with our comprehensive strength training programs. We offer both free weights and machine-based exercises.

Cardio Workouts

Improve your cardiovascular health with our wide range of cardio equipment, including treadmills, ellipticals, and stationary bikes.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Our HIIT classes are designed to burn maximum calories in a short amount of time, combining intense bursts of activity with short recovery periods.

Yoga and Pilates

Enhance your flexibility, balance, and core strength with our yoga and Pilates classes. Suitable for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

Functional Training

Train your body for everyday activities with functional training exercises that improve your strength, coordination, and balance.

CrossFit

Join our CrossFit classes for a high-intensity workout that combines elements of cardio, weightlifting, and bodyweight exercises.
